National Stadium

The National Stadium is the Pièce De Résistance of the Singapore Sports Hub. It was completed in late 2013 and officially opened on 30th June 2014. The stadium features the world's largest retractable domed roof and configurable seating on the lowest spectator tier to make it the only stadium in the world that is custom-designed to host different sporting events at any given time.


This allows for various types of events to be held there while also allowing for those who visit the area to enjoy spectacular views of the waterfront and the Singapore city skyline.

Some examples of the events that were held there include:

  • Track and Field Finals


  • Spartan Race


  • BTS Concert


And many more!

OCBC Arena

Along with the National Stadium, the OCBC Arena also stands out in its own way. It comprises of two blocks of multi-purpose halls and can accomodate a full range of sporting activites. The best part about this is that it is open to the public! As long as there is a booking made, anyone can come here to enjoy the facilities and have fun.


Some examples of the sporting activities that can be done here include:

  1. Basketball


  2. Badminton


  3. Volleyball
